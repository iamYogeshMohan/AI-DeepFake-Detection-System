About The Project

The Deepfake Problem

AI-generated fake media is increasingly used for misinformation, identity theft, and fraud. Distinguishing real from manipulated content is a critical challenge today.

Enter DeepGuard

DeepGuard is a state-of-the-art detection system designed to combat digital forgery by identifying subtle artifacts left behind by generative AI tools.

How It Works

By leveraging deep learning models, specifically Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), the system analyzes facial patterns and pixel inconsistencies to verify media authenticity.
